Our Mission

Ekklesia Agora is a gathering place for Eastern Orthodox Christians — a digital Agora where the faithful can find authentic Orthodox video content, share in communal life, and be formed in the Holy Tradition passed down from the Apostles.

In a world of noise and distraction, we offer a sacred space: rooted in Scripture, guided by the Fathers, and centered on the Eucharistic life of the Church.

"Where two or three are gathered in My name, there am I in the midst of them."— Matthew 18:20
☩

Orthodox Only

Every video, every voice, every teaching here is firmly rooted in Eastern Orthodox Christianity. No compromises. Holy Tradition preserved.

🏛

A Living Community

More than a video platform — a community. Share reflections, ask questions, pray together, and grow in the life of the Church.

🕯

Formed by the Fathers

Our content draws from the inexhaustible well of patristic wisdom — St. John Chrysostom, St. Basil the Great, St. Gregory Palamas, and the whole cloud of witnesses.

✦

Open to Inquirers

Curious about Orthodoxy? Come and see, as the Apostle Philip once said. All sincere seekers are welcome here.

Built on PeerTube

Ekklesia Agora is built on PeerTube — a free, open-source, decentralized video platform. No algorithms, no advertising, no corporate control over sacred content.
