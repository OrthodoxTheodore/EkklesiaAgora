Our Mission

Ekklesia Agora is a gathering place for Eastern Orthodox Christians — a digital Agora where the faithful can find authentic Orthodox video content, share in communal life, and be formed in the Holy Tradition passed down from the Apostles.

"Where two or three are gathered in My name, there am I in the midst of them."— Matthew 18:20
☩

Orthodox Only

Every video, every voice, every teaching here is firmly rooted in Eastern Orthodox Christianity. No compromises. No confusion. Holy Tradition preserved.

🏛

A Living Community

More than a video platform — a community. Share reflections, ask questions, pray together, and grow in the life of the Church.

🕯

Formed by the Fathers

Our content draws from the inexhaustible well of patristic wisdom — the Holy Fathers and the whole cloud of witnesses.

✦

Open to Inquirers

Curious about Orthodoxy? Come and see, as the Apostle Philip once said. All sincere seekers are welcome.

Built on PeerTube

Ekklesia Agora is built on PeerTube — a free, open-source, decentralized video platform. No algorithms, no advertising, no corporate control over sacred content.
